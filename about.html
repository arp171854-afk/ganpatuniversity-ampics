About AMPICS

A Legacy of Excellence in Technology Education Since 1999

Our Institute Overview

Acharya Motibhai Patel Institute of Computer Studies (AMPICS) is situated on the sprawling Ganpat Vidyanagar campus along the Mehsana-Gozaria Highway in Gujarat. Established in 1999, AMPICS stands proudly as a premier private institute dedicated to advancing computer studies and technology education in the region.

Affiliated with Ganpat University and formally accredited by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), the institute is built on a foundation of rigorous teaching pedagogy. Beyond standard academics, AMPICS is recognized as a leader in research, innovation, and holistic student development.

Great emphasis is placed on activities beyond the classroom. This is heavily supported by our state-of-the-art infrastructure, vibrant extracurricular initiatives, and a robust network of national and international collaborations. Our placement opportunities are highly varied, ensuring students find dynamic roles in the corporate sector, public sector, or embark on their own journeys in entrepreneurship.
