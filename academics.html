Department of Computer Applications

Explore our comprehensive degree programs designed to build the next generation of IT leaders. Below you will find detailed information regarding course structure, duration, eligibility, and the annual fee structure.

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA Hons.)

A rigorous program focusing on modern software engineering, web development (HTML, CSS, JS), and foundational computer science logic.

Duration

3 Years (Regular) / 4 Years (Honors)

Eligibility

10+2 (Any Stream) from a recognized board.

Annual Tuition Fee

₹ 70,000 / Year

Key Subjects (Sem 2)

Web Dev, Logic Programming, DBMS

Master of Computer Applications (MCA)

An advanced postgraduate degree preparing students for high-level enterprise architecture, artificial intelligence, and corporate IT leadership.

Duration

2 Years (4 Semesters)

Eligibility

BCA/B.Sc (IT) or equivalent degree.

Annual Tuition Fee

₹ 1,40,000 / Year

Career Prospects

Software Engineer, Data Scientist, System Analyst

Integrated BCA + MCA

A seamless 5-year journey from foundational coding to advanced application architecture without the hassle of reapplying for a master's program.

Duration

5 Years (10 Semesters)

Eligibility

10+2 with Mathematics/Computer Science.

Annual Tuition Fee

₹ 1,05,000 / Year

Advantage

Saves 1 year compared to standard BCA + MCA.

Ph.D. in Computer Science

For scholars dedicated to advanced research in computing technologies, AI, machine learning, and data architecture.

Duration

3 to 5 Years

Eligibility

MCA/M.Tech/M.Sc in relevant field.
