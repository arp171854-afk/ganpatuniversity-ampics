Life at GUNI

300 Acres of Learning, Innovation, and Growth

A Lush Green Ecosystem

The Ganpat Vidyanagar campus is a sprawling 300-acre green haven. Designed to provide a serene and pollution-free environment, the campus features extensive tree plantations, manicured gardens, and open green spaces perfect for outdoor study sessions and relaxation.

Our commitment to sustainability includes green campus initiatives, solar energy adoption, and organized tree-planting drives, creating an eco-friendly atmosphere that enhances focus and well-being.

Championship Sports Facilities

Physical fitness is a core part of the student experience at AMPICS. The university boasts world-class sporting infrastructure to help students excel outside the classroom.

Facilities include a massive cricket ground, football fields, basketball courts, and a fully equipped indoor gymnasium. We regularly host inter-college tournaments and athletic meets, fostering teamwork, leadership, and university pride.

Culture & Community Impact

Campus life is highly energetic, driven by dynamic student initiatives and massive cultural festivals. The flagship Yuvarangat Navratri Celebration brings thousands of students together for epic nights of Garba, fostering deep cultural roots.

Beyond celebrations, students actively participate in social outreach. Through the National Service Scheme (NSS) camps and voluntary movements, students engage in community service, rural development, and health awareness drives, building strong leadership and empathy.
